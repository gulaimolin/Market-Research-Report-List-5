<p><ul><li>Full Container Load (FCL)</li><li>Less-than Container Load (LCL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Land Freight Forwarding market can be categorized into two main types: Full Container Load (FCL) and Less-than Container Load (LCL) markets. In the FCL market, a single customer occupies an entire container to ship their goods, while in the LCL market, multiple customers share space within a single container. FCL is typically more cost-effective for large shipments, while LCL is preferred for smaller shipments that do not require a full container. Both markets provide efficient transportation solutions for land freight forwarding.</p></p>
