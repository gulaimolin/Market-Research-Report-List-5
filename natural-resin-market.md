<p><p>Natural resin market is divided into three main types - Rosin Resin, Shellac Resin, and Others. Rosin Resin is derived from pine trees and is commonly used in adhesives, varnishes, and inks. Shellac Resin, derived from the secretions of the lac insect, is used in wood finishes, sealants, and pharmaceutical coatings. The Others category encompasses a variety of natural resins such as dammar, copal, and mastic, which have various industrial applications including in food packaging, perfumes, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
