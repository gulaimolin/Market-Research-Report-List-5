<p><p>Photonic Crystals market can be classified into three types based on their dimensional structure: Three-Dimensional Photonic Crystals, Two-Dimensional Photonic Crystals, and One-Dimensional Photonic Crystals. Three-Dimensional Photonic Crystals have a periodic nanostructure in three dimensions, providing unique optical properties. Two-Dimensional Photonic Crystals have a nanostructure in two dimensions, offering control over light propagation. One-Dimensional Photonic Crystals have periodicity in one dimension, enabling manipulation of light at specific wavelengths. Each type offers distinct advantages for various applications in the photonics industry.</p></p>
