<p><p>Cored wire market includes various types such as Calcium Silicide Cored Wire (CaSi), Calcium Solid Cored Wire (Ca), Pure Carbon Cored Wire (C), Ferro Calcium Cored Wire (CaFe), and others. Calcium Silicide Cored Wire (CaSi) is used for deoxidation and desulfurization in steelmaking. Calcium Solid Cored Wire (Ca) is used for calcium treatment in steel production. Pure Carbon Cored Wire (C) is used for carbon injection in steelmaking. Ferro Calcium Cored Wire (CaFe) is utilized for calcium and iron alloy addition in steel manufacturing. Other types cater to specific requirements in the metal industry.</p></p>
