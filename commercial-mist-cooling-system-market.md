<p><strong>The Commercial Mist Cooling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Mist Cooling System</li><li>Medium Pressure Mist Cooling System</li><li>High Pressure Mist Cooling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial mist cooling systems are categorized into low, medium, and high-pressure systems based on the amount of pressure they operate at. Low-pressure systems typically operate at pressures below 250 psi, making them more affordable but less effective at cooling large areas. Medium-pressure systems operate between 250-1000 psi, striking a balance between cost and effectiveness. High-pressure systems operate above 1000 psi and offer the most efficient cooling for large commercial spaces, but come at a higher cost. Each type of system caters to different cooling needs and budgets in commercial settings.</p></p>
