<p><p>Phase balance relays are used in both single phase and three phase systems to monitor the balance of voltage or current between phases. In a single phase system, the relay compares the voltage or current of the single phase to a reference value to determine if the system is balanced. In a three phase system, the relay monitors the balance between all three phases to ensure that no phase is overloaded or underloaded, which can lead to equipment damage or power outages.</p></p>
