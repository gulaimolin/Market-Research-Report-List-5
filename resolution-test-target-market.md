<p><p>Resolution test targets are used in industries such as optics, photography, and imaging to evaluate the resolution capabilities of devices such as cameras and microscopes. Star targets feature a star-shaped pattern to assess resolution and distortion. Sinusoidal targets have varying frequencies to measure resolution across different scales. Variable frequency targets allow for customization of resolution testing. USAF Pattern Wheel Targets have patterns of decreasing sizes to determine the ability to resolve fine details. Resolving Power Charts display resolution capabilities at various distances. IEEE targets cater to specific markets by offering standardized testing methods for resolution assessment.</p></p>
