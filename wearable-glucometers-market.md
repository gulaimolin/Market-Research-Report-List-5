<p><p>Wearable glucometers are devices used to monitor blood sugar levels continuously. They come in various types such as smartwatches, wristbands, skin patches, smart shoes and socks, and contact lenses. Smartwatches and wristbands are worn on the wrist and provide real-time data. Skin patches are adhesive sensors placed on the skin to measure glucose levels. Smart shoes and socks have built-in sensors to track glucose levels. Contact lenses have sensors that measure glucose in tears, providing a non-invasive monitoring option.</p></p>
