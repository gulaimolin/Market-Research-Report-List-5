<p><p>Galvanized Spring Steel Wire refers to steel wire that has been coated with a layer of zinc in order to prevent corrosion and increase durability. There are two main types of Galvanized Spring Steel Wire markets - Electro-Galvanizing Steel Wire and Hot-Dip Galvanized Steel Wire. In the Electro-Galvanizing process, the steel wire is coated with zinc through an electrolytic process, while in the Hot-Dip Galvanizing process, the steel wire is dipped into a bath of molten zinc. Both types of coatings provide excellent protection against corrosion and rust.</p></p>
