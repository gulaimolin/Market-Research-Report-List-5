<p><p>Cycling gas turbine markets can be categorized into two main types: Open Loop and Closed Loop systems. In an Open Loop system, the turbine operates continuously, with varying load levels depending on demand. In contrast, a Closed Loop system has the ability to turn the turbine on and off in response to changes in demand, allowing for more flexibility in operation. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, depending on factors such as energy efficiency, cost, and environmental impact.</p></p>
