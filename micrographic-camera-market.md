Electron micrographic cameras, on the other hand, use electron beams to capture images at even higher magnifications than optical micrographs. These cameras are used in fields such as nanotechnology, semiconductor research, and metallurgy. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in scientific research and analysis.</p></p>
