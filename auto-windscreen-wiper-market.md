<p><p>Auto windscreen wipers are available in two main types: boneless wipers and bone wipers. Boneless wipers have a sleeker design and are known for their improved performance in clearing the windshield of water and debris. They use a flexible blade that conforms to the curve of the windscreen, providing consistent pressure for better visibility. On the other hand, bone wipers have a traditional metal frame and rubber blade design. They are a more cost-effective option but may not provide as smooth and efficient wiping action as boneless wipers.</p></p>
