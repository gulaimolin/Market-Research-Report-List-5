<p><ul><li>Double Thread</li><li>Parallel Lines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible couplings market includes various types such as Double Thread and Parallel Lines. Double Thread couplings are designed with threads on both ends, allowing for easy installation and alignment of shafts. This type is ideal for applications requiring a high level of precision and torque transmission. On the other hand, Parallel Lines couplings have parallel shafts and are used to connect two shafts in a straight line. They provide flexibility and shock absorption, making them suitable for use in a wide range of industries.</p></p>
