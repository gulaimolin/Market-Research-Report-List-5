<p><p>Semiconductor photoinitiators are crucial components in photolithography processes. They can be classified into two main types: Ionic PAG and Non-Ionic PAG. Ionic PAGs contain a positively charged ion that helps in initiating the photopolymerization process, while Non-Ionic PAGs do not contain any charged ions. Both types of photoinitiators play a key role in initiating the crosslinking of polymers in semiconductor manufacturing processes, leading to the creation of intricate patterns on semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
