<p><ul><li>Synchronous Boost Converter</li><li>Non-synchronous Boost Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boost converter market can be divided into two main types: Synchronous Boost Converter and Non-synchronous Boost Converter. Synchronous Boost Converters use a power switch and a synchronous rectifier to control the flow of energy, resulting in higher efficiency and lower heat dissipation. On the other hand, Non-synchronous boost converters do not utilize a synchronous rectifier, resulting in a simpler design but lower efficiency. Both types cater to different consumer needs based on factors such as cost, efficiency, and design complexity.</p></p>
