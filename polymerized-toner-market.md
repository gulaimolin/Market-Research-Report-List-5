<p><p>Polymerized Toner is classified into two main types: Positive Polymerized Toner and Passive Polymerized Toner. Positive Polymerized Toner is designed to be positively charged, making it more efficient in attracting negatively charged particles during the printing process. On the other hand, Passive Polymerized Toner is neutral in charge, requiring less energy and reducing waste during printing. Both types offer unique advantages in terms of print quality, energy efficiency, and environmental impact, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
