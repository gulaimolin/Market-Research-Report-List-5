<p><p>Fume exhaust systems market consists of different types of components like collection hoods, ducting, air cleaning devices, blowers, and fans. Collection hoods are used to capture harmful fumes at the source, while ducting helps in transporting these fumes to the air cleaning device. The air cleaning device helps in removing pollutants from the fumes before releasing them into the environment. Blowers and fans are used to create the necessary airflow to suck in the fumes and push them out of the system.</p></p>
