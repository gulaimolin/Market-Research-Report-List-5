<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Xenograft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone graft alternatives market includes allograft and xenograft options. Allograft refers to using bone tissue from a donor of the same species, typically human, to promote bone growth and healing in a recipient. Xenograft involves using bone tissue from a donor of a different species, such as bovine or porcine, for the same purpose. Both allograft and xenograft offer viable alternatives to traditional autografts, providing options for patients who may not have suitable donor sites or wish to avoid additional surgery to harvest bone tissue.</p></p>
