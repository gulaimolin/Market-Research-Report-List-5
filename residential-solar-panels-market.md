<p><p>Residential solar panels can be classified into different types based on the type of materials used in their construction. Monocrystalline solar panels are made from single-crystal silicon, while polycrystalline solar panels are made from multiple silicon crystals. Thin-film amorphous solar panels are made using thin layers of various materials like silicon, cadmium, and copper. Other types of residential solar panels may include organic photovoltaic cells or concentrated solar panels. Each type of solar panel offers its own unique advantages in terms of efficiency, cost, and durability.</p></p>
