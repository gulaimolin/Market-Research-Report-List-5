<p><p>Single-rotor and double-rotor helicopters are the two main market types in the emergency helicopter industry. Single-rotor helicopters have one main rotor system, offering simplicity and ease of control. They are commonly used for medical emergencies and search and rescue operations. On the other hand, double-rotor helicopters feature two main rotor systems that provide better stability and lifting capacity, making them suitable for transporting larger numbers of patients or personnel in emergency situations. Both types play a crucial role in providing rapid and efficient emergency response services.</p></p>
