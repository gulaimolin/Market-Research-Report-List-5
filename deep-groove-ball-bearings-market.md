<p><p>Deep-groove ball bearings are classified into single-row, double-row, and multi-row bearings based on the number of rows of balls in the bearing. Single-row bearings are the most common type, offering high speed and radial load capacities. Double-row bearings have higher load capacity and stiffness compared to single-row bearings. Multi-row bearings have even higher load capacities and are used in heavy load applications. Each type of bearing is designed to accommodate different levels of loads and speeds, catering to various industrial applications.</p></p>
