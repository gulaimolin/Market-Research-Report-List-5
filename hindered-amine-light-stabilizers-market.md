<p><p>Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) are classified into three types based on their chemical structure: Polymeric Type, Monomeric Type, and Oligomeric Type. Polymeric HALS are larger molecules with multiple amine groups, providing efficient long-term UV protection. Monomeric HALS are smaller molecules that readily migrate to the surface of polymers, offering immediate UV protection. Oligomeric HALS are medium-sized molecules that provide a balance between immediate and long-term UV stabilization. Each type offers unique benefits for enhancing the durability of various materials exposed to outdoor conditions.</p></p>
