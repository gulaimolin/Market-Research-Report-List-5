<p><p>Phosphorescent tapes are available in two main market types: BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride). BOPP phosphorescent tapes are known for their durability and resistance to tearing, making them ideal for long-term use. On the other hand, PVC phosphorescent tapes are known for their flexibility, making them suitable for curved surfaces and irregular shapes. Both types of phosphorescent tapes are commonly used for safety and emergency signage, as well as in artistic and decorative applications.</p></p>
