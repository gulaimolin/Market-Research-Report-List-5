<p><p>Electroactive Polymers can be classified into two main types: Electronic Type Electroactive Polymers and Ionic Type Electroactive Polymers. Electronic Type Electroactive Polymers are those that change their shape or size in response to an electrical stimulus, making them suitable for applications in actuators and sensors. On the other hand, Ionic Type Electroactive Polymers rely on the migration of ions within their structure to exhibit electroactivity, making them suitable for applications such as artificial muscles and energy harvesting devices. Both types play key roles in the growing Electroactive Polymers market.</p></p>
