<p><p>Continuous heating furnaces are essential for industrial processes such as forging, annealing, and heat treating. The Rotary-Hearth Type furnace consists of a rotating hearth that allows for uniform heating of materials as they move through the furnace. On the other hand, the Straight-Chamber Type furnace features a stationary chamber where materials are continuously fed in and heated before exiting. Both types of furnaces are used in various industries to efficiently heat materials for production processes.</p></p>
