<p><p>Laboratory Distillation Systems come in various types to suit different applications. Distillation is the most basic form, separating substances based on boiling points. Steam distillation uses steam to separate volatile compounds. Vacuum distillation lowers the boiling points by reducing pressure. Fractional distillation separates compounds with closer boiling points by using a column with multiple trays. Each type of distillation system caters to specific needs in laboratories, offering precise separation and purification of substances.</p></p>
