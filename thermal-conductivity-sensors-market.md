<p><p>Thermal conductivity sensors market can be classified into two types - Type I and Type II. Type I market includes sensors that measure thermal conductivity based on the heat flow through a material. These sensors are commonly used in industrial applications for determining the thermal properties of materials. On the other hand, Type II market consists of sensors that operate based on the change in electrical conductance in response to temperature variations. These sensors are often used in consumer electronics and environmental monitoring applications.</p></p>
