<p><p>Dermal adhesives are used in wound closure and surgical procedures to promote healing and prevent infections. There are two main types of dermal adhesives: synthetic and natural. Synthetic dermal adhesives are made from chemical compounds and provide strong adhesion properties. Natural dermal adhesives, on the other hand, are derived from natural sources such as proteins and sugars, offering a biocompatible option for wound closure.
