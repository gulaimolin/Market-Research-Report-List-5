The market is also benefiting from advancements in energy-saving technologies and increasing demand for sustainable building solutions.</p><p>In addition, the EPC market is witnessing a shift towards performance-based contracts, where ESCOs are paid based on the actual energy savings achieved, rather than just the implementation of measures.
