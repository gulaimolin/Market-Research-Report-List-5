<p><ul><li>Single Automatic Operation</li><li>Selective Collective Operation</li><li>Group Automatic Operation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elevator Integrated Controller Market includes three main types: Single Automatic Operation, Selective Collective Operation, and Group Automatic Operation. Single Automatic Operation enables individual control over elevator movement. Selective Collective Operation optimizes elevator efficiency by grouping passengers with similar destinations. Group Automatic Operation coordinates multiple elevators in a building to efficiently transport passengers. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, offering a range of control options for elevator systems. The market for these controllers continues to evolve as technology advances and buildings require more sophisticated elevator systems.</p></p>
