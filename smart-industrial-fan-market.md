<p><p>Centrifugal fans and axial fans are two common types of smart industrial fans in the market. Centrifugal fans are designed to increase pressure and move air radially, making them ideal for applications that require high air flow and resistance to changes in airflow resistance. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air in a straight line parallel to the fan's axis, making them more suitable for applications that require moving large volumes of air at low pressures. Both types of fans play crucial roles in industrial ventilation and cooling systems.</p></p>
