<p><p>Fragrances microencapsulation market types include physico-mechanical methods, chemical methods, and physico-chemical methods. Physico-mechanical methods involve techniques like spray drying and fluidized bed coating to encapsulate fragrances. Chemical methods use processes such as coacervation and interfacial polymerization to create microcapsules. Physico-chemical methods combine both physical and chemical techniques to achieve encapsulation. Each of these methods offers unique benefits and advantages for the fragrance industry, allowing for controlled release, improved stability, and enhanced scent diffusion in various products.</p></p>
