<p><p>Centralized dust collectors are used in industrial settings to capture and filter out dust and other particles from the air. The three main types of centralized dust collectors are cartridge pulse, bag pulse, and reverse air. Cartridge pulse collectors use pleated filters to capture dust, while bag pulse collectors use fabric bags. Reverse air collectors use a reverse airflow to clean the filters. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs and requirements of the industrial application.</p></p>
