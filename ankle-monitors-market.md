<p><p>Ankle monitors, used for monitoring individuals on probation or parole, come in two main types: Radio Frequency (RF) and Global Positioning System (GPS). RF ankle monitors use radio waves to communicate with a receiver installed in the individual's home, tracking their movements within a certain range. GPS ankle monitors, on the other hand, use satellite technology to provide real-time location tracking, allowing for more precise monitoring of an individual's movements both indoors and outdoors.</p></p>
