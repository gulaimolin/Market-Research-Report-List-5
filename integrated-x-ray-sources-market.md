<p><p>Integrated X-ray sources come in two main types: Open Type and Sealed Type. Open Type sources allow for easy access to the X-ray tube, making maintenance and repairs simpler. On the other hand, Sealed Type sources are completely enclosed, providing better radiation protection and longer lifespan. Both types have their advantages depending on the specific application and requirements of the user. The choice between Open Type and Sealed Type integrated X-ray sources will depend on factors such as usage frequency, budget, and safety considerations.</p></p>
