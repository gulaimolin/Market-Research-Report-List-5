<p><p>Fiber Bragg Grating reflectors are used in both single mode fiber and double-clad fiber markets. Single mode fiber market utilizes fibers with a small core size, allowing for a single mode of light to propagate through the fiber. Double-clad fiber market, on the other hand, uses fibers with an inner core surrounded by a larger outer cladding layer. This configuration enables higher power handling capabilities and is commonly used in fiber lasers and amplifiers.</p></p>
