<p><p>Point-of-use water purifiers come in various types to suit different needs. Gravity purifiers rely on gravity to filter out impurities, making them suitable for areas with limited access to electricity. UV purifiers use ultraviolet light to disinfect water and are effective against bacteria and viruses. RO purifiers use a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants and are ideal for areas with high levels of dissolved solids. Each type offers unique advantages for providing safe and clean drinking water.</p></p>
