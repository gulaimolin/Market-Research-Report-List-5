<p><strong>The Industrial Oil Water Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Separation (Mechanical Methods, etc.)</li><li>Chemical Separation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Physical separation in the industrial oil water separator market involves using mechanical methods such as gravity, centrifugal force, and filtration to separate oil from water. This type of separation is based on the physical properties of the substances. On the other hand, chemical separation involves using chemical agents such as coagulants, flocculants, and oil dispersants to change the chemical properties of the oil and water, allowing them to separate easily. Both types of separation methods are widely used in industrial applications for effective oil-water separation.</p></p>
