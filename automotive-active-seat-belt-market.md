<p><p>Automotive active seat belts come in various types to provide different levels of safety and comfort for passengers. Two-point belts offer basic restraint, while three-point belts add a shoulder strap for additional protection. Four-point belts provide even more support by including attachments at the hips. Five-point belts offer the highest level of protection with additional attachments at the chest. Six-point and seven-point belts are typically used in racing cars to ensure maximum security during high-speed impacts. Each type caters to specific safety needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
