<p><p>Liquid-Based Needle Free Injector Market Types include Jet, Spring, Laser, and Vibration technologies. Jet injectors use high-pressure liquid streams to penetrate the skin. Spring injectors use a spring mechanism to deliver the liquid medication. Laser injectors use laser technology to create micropores in the skin for drug delivery. Vibration injectors utilize high-frequency vibrations to push the liquid through the skin. Each technology offers a unique method of delivering medication without the need for needles, catering to various preferences and requirements in the medical field.</p></p>
