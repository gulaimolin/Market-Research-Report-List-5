<p><p>Dried egg powder market can be categorized into three main types: whole egg powder, egg white powder, and egg yolk powder. Whole egg powder is made by spray drying a mixture of egg whites and yolks, providing a convenient alternative to fresh eggs. Egg white powder is made by drying just the egg whites, commonly used in baking and protein shakes. Egg yolk powder is made by drying the yolks only, often used in making sauces and mayonnaise. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
