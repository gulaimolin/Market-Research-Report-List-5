Parylene N is known for its general purpose coating, Parylene C for its high dielectric strength, Parylene D for its lubricity and biocompatibility, Parylene F for its increased thickness, Parylene HT for its ability to withstand high temperatures, while other types offer unique properties for specific needs.</p></p>
