<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Pipe</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold Rolled Plate market includes different types of products such as Spiral Weld Pipe, Electric Resistance Weld, and Double Submerged Arc Weld. Spiral Weld Pipe is formed by rolling a steel plate and welding the edges together to create a cylindrical pipe. Electric Resistance Weld pipes are made by passing a high-frequency electrical current through the steel strip, creating a weld seam. Double Submerged Arc Weld pipes are formed by welding two longitudinal seams simultaneously using submerged arc welding process.</p></p>
