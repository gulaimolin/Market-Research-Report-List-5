<p><p>Pretreatment auxiliaries are chemicals used in various stages of textile processing to improve the efficiency of subsequent treatments. The types of pretreatment auxiliaries include activator agents, which enhance the performance of other chemicals; desizing agents, which remove sizing agents from textiles; dispersant agents, which help disperse dye molecules evenly; cleaner agents, which remove impurities from fabrics. Other types of pretreatment auxiliaries include sequestrants, wetting agents, and emulsifiers. Each type plays a unique role in preparing textiles for various treatments and finishes.</p></p>
