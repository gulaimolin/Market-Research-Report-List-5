<p><p>Purging compounds are used in various industries to clean and remove residue from machinery and equipment. There are three main types of purging compounds: Mechanical Purging Compounds, which are abrasive and help scrub away residue, Chemical/Foaming Purging Compounds, which use chemical reactions to break down and remove stubborn residues, and Liquid Purging Compounds, which are effective at cleaning molds and other hard-to-reach areas.
