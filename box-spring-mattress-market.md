<p><ul><li>Linked Spring</li><li>One-Line Rigid Spring</li><li>Independent Canister Spring</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Box spring mattresses are available in different market types including linked spring, one-line rigid spring, independent canister spring, and others. Linked spring mattresses have coils connected in a row, providing support across the entire mattress. One-line rigid spring mattresses have coils arranged in a single line, offering firmer support. Independent canister spring mattresses have individual coils enclosed in fabric pockets, providing personalized support. Other types of box spring mattresses may include variations such as hybrid mattresses or memory foam options.</p></p>
