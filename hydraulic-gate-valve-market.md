<p><p>Hydraulic gate valves are categorized into different types based on the design of the gate. Solid wedge gate valves have a solid gate that provides strength and stability. Flexible wedge gate valves have a gate that can adjust to changes in temperature and pressure. Split wedge gate valves have a two-piece gate for improved sealing. Parallel disk gate valves have gates that move parallel to the flow of the fluid. Each type of gate valve is suited for different applications depending on the specific needs of the system.</p></p>
