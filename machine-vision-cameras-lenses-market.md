<p><ul><li>Fixed Focal Lens</li><li>Zoom Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine vision cameras lenses are crucial components of machine vision systems. There are two main types of lenses in the market: Fixed Focal Lens and Zoom Lens. Fixed focal lenses have a set focal length, providing a fixed field of view and depth of field. They are ideal for applications where the distance between the camera and the object is known and constant. On the other hand, zoom lenses allow users to adjust the focal length, providing flexibility in changing the field of view and magnification level as needed.</p></p>
