<p><p>Semi-Anechoic Chambers are used for testing electromagnetic compatibility and other noise-related measurements. There are three main types of market available - Standard Wedge, Perforated Metallic Wedge, and Sound Absorbing Wedge. Standard Wedge chambers have solid walls and ceilings for absorbing sound waves. Perforated Metallic Wedge chambers use metal wedges with perforations to absorb sound. Sound Absorbing Wedge chambers have wedge-shaped absorbers made of materials like foam or fiberglass to dampen sound reflections. Each type of chamber offers unique advantages for specific testing needs.</p></p>
