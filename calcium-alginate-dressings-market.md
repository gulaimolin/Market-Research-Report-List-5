<p><p>Calcium Alginate Dressings are available in two main types - Gauze Calcium Alginate Dressings and Poly Blend Calcium Alginate Dressings. Gauze Calcium Alginate Dressings are made of a non-woven material that provides absorbency and conformability, making them ideal for moderate to heavily exuding wounds. Poly Blend Calcium Alginate Dressings, on the other hand, are a combination of alginate fibers and other materials like carboxymethylcellulose, providing additional strength and stability. Both types of dressings help promote wound healing by creating a moist environment and facilitating the removal of necrotic tissue.</p></p>
