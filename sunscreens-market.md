<p><ul><li>Chemical Sunscreens</li><li>Physical Sunscreens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical sunscreens work by absorbing UV rays and converting them into heat, while physical sunscreens create a barrier on the skin that reflects UV rays. Chemical sunscreens are popular for their lightweight and easy-to-spread texture, while physical sunscreens are preferred for their gentle and non-irritating formula. Both types of sunscreens offer protection against the sun's harmful rays, but some people may have sensitivities to certain chemicals in chemical sunscreens, making physical sunscreens a better choice for sensitive skin.</p></p>
