<p><p>Loan origination tools are available in two main market types: on-demand (cloud) and on-premise. On-demand, or cloud-based loan origination tools, are hosted on the provider's servers and accessed through the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premise loan origination tools are installed and operated on the client's own servers and infrastructure. While they offer more control and customization options, they require higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences of lenders.</p></p>
