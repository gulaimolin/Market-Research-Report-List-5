<p><p>The audiology devices market consists of various types of devices tailored to address different hearing needs. Hearing aids are the most common devices used to amplify sound for individuals with hearing loss. Cochlear implants are surgically implanted devices that stimulate the auditory nerve for those with severe hearing loss. Diagnostic devices are used to assess and diagnose hearing loss. Bone Anchored Hearing Aids (BAHA) are implanted devices that transmit sound vibrations through the skull bone for individuals with specific types of hearing loss.</p></p>
