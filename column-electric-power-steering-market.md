<p><p>Column-Electric Power Steering market types include Brushed Motor and Brushless Motor. Brushed Motor steering systems use brushes to deliver power, making them more affordable but requiring more maintenance. In contrast, Brushless Motor systems are more advanced and efficient as they do not require brushes, resulting in improved durability and performance. While Brushed Motor systems are commonly used in older vehicles for cost-effectiveness, Brushless Motor systems are becoming increasingly popular in modern vehicles for their superior performance and reliability.</p></p>
