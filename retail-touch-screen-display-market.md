<p><strong>The Retail Touch Screen Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Touch Screen</li><li>Capacitive Touch Screen</li><li>Infrared Touch Screen</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Retail touch screen displays come in various types, including resistive, capacitive, and infrared touch screens. Resistive touch screens are pressure-sensitive and cost-effective, while capacitive touch screens rely on the electrical charge of the human body for operation. Infrared touch screens use infrared technology to detect touch and are durable for high-traffic environments. Other types of touch screens include surface acoustic wave and optical imaging, each offering unique features and functionalities to cater to different retail needs.</p></p>
