<p><p>Strawberry puree comes in two main types: natural and composite. Natural puree is made solely from strawberries, without the addition of any other ingredients. This type is favored by consumers looking for a pure and simple product. On the other hand, composite puree is made by blending strawberries with other fruits or additives to create unique flavors and textures. This type appeals to consumers looking for more complex and interesting options in their puree products. Both types cater to different preferences in the market.</p></p>
