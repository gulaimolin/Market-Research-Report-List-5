<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Motion Checkweighers are designed to weigh products as they move along a production line, allowing for high-speed weighing and continuous operation. Intermittent Checkweighers, on the other hand, weigh products in batches or at specific intervals, suited for slower production speeds or when products need to be stopped for weighing. Both types of checkweigher systems are used in various industries to ensure products meet weight requirements, prevent costly overfilling or underfilling, and maintain quality control standards.</p></p>
