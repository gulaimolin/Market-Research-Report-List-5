<p><p>The commercial toilet tank fittings market can be broadly classified into four types: Fill Valve, Flush Valve, Push Button & Lever, and Others. Fill valve is responsible for refilling the tank with water after each flush. Flush valve controls the release of water to clean the bowl. Push Button & Lever are mechanisms used to initiate the flushing process. Other types of fittings include components like gaskets, seals, and mounting hardware that are essential for the proper functioning of the toilet tank.</p></p>
