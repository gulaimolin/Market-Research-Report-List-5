<p><p>Optical energy meters are categorized into different types based on their technology, including pyroelectric, photodiode, and others. Pyroelectric optical energy meters are based on the pyroelectric effect, which generates an electric signal in response to temperature changes caused by light. Photodiode energy meters use photodiodes to convert light into electrical current for energy measurement. Other types of optical energy meters may utilize different technologies such as thermal sensors or thermopiles. Each type of meter offers unique advantages and is suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
