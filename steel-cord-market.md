<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Steel Cord Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Tensile (NT)</li><li>High Tensile (HT)</li><li>Super Tensile (ST)</li><li>Ultra Tensile (UT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel cord market types are categorized based on their tensile strength. Normal Tensile (NT) steel cords have a lower tensile strength, suitable for general applications. High Tensile (HT) steel cords have increased strength and are used for heavier-duty applications. Super Tensile (ST) cords have even higher strength than HT, ideal for specialized industries. Ultra Tensile (UT) cords offer the highest tensile strength and are used in extreme conditions where maximum durability is required. Different types cater to varying needs in the market.</p></p>
