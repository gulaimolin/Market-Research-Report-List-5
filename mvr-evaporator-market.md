<p><strong>The MVR Evaporator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Effect Evaporation</li><li>Vapor Recompression</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MVR Evaporator Market encompasses two main types: Multi-Effect Evaporation and Vapor Recompression. Multi-Effect Evaporation involves multiple stages of evaporation, where the vapor from one effect is used to heat the next effect, increasing overall efficiency. Vapor Recompression, on the other hand, uses a compressor to increase the pressure of the vapor, allowing for more efficient heat transfer and reducing energy consumption. Both types of evaporators are popular choices in industries where high energy efficiency is crucial.</p></p>
