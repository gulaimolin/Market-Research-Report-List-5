<p><p>Polyamide 12 can be produced through two main methods in the market: polycondensation method and opening polymerization method. The polycondensation method involves the reaction of diamine and diacid monomers, leading to the formation of a long chain polymer. On the other hand, the opening polymerization method involves the reaction of lactams which results in the formation of the polyamide 12 polymer. Both methods are widely used in the market to produce different types of Polyamide 12 for various applications.</p></p>
