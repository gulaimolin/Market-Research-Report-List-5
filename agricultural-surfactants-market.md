<p><p>Agricultural surfactants play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency of pesticides and herbicides by reducing surface tension and aiding in the spreading and penetration of these chemicals. There are four main types of agricultural surfactants: non-ionic, anionic, cationic, and amphoteric. Non-ionic surfactants are versatile and compatible with most pesticides, anionic surfactants are effective at lowering surface tension, cationic surfactants have a positive charge and can improve adhesion, and amphoteric surfactants can be used in a wide range of pH levels. Each type serves specific functions in improving agricultural applications.</p></p>
