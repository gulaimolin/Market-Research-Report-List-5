<p><p>Robotics and Automation Actuators are devices used to control and move machinery and equipment in various industries. There are three main types of actuators: electric actuators, pneumatic actuators, and hydraulic actuators. Electric actuators use electricity to generate motion, pneumatic actuators use compressed air, and hydraulic actuators use fluid pressure. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with electric actuators being precise and efficient, pneumatic actuators being cost-effective and easy to maintain, and hydraulic actuators being powerful and capable of handling heavy loads.</p></p>
