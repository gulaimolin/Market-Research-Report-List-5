<p><p>Backlight modules are essential components in displays to provide uniform lighting for better visibility. There are two main types of backlight modules - CCFL (cold cathode fluorescent lamp) and LED (light-emitting diode). CCFL backlight modules are cost-effective and widely used in LCD displays. On the other hand, LED backlight modules are becoming increasingly popular due to their energy efficiency and longer lifespan. LED backlight modules also offer better color reproduction and thinner designs, making them the preferred choice for many modern display applications.</p></p>
