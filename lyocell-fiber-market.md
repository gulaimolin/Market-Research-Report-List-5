<p><strong>The Lyocell Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular Lyocell Fiber</li><li>Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regular Lyocell Fiber is a type of fiber made from natural cellulose found in wood pulp. It is known for its softness, strength, and moisture-wicking properties, making it ideal for clothing and textiles. Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber, on the other hand, is a modified version of regular Lyocell Fiber that has been chemically treated to improve its durability and wrinkle resistance. This type of fiber is often used in applications where increased strength and shape retention are desired, such as in upholstery and home textiles.</p></p>
