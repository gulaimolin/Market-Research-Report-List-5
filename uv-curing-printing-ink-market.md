<p><p>UV curing printing ink market types include arc curing and LED curing. Arc curing involves using a high-intensity arc lamp to cure the ink, providing fast curing times and high energy output. LED curing, on the other hand, uses light-emitting diodes to cure the ink gradually and energy-efficiently. Both methods offer advantages in terms of speed, energy consumption, and curing capabilities, catering to different printing needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
