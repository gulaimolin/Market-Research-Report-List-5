<p><p>Tube extrusion lines are used for manufacturing plastic tubes for various industries. There are two main types of tube extrusion lines: single-layer and multi-layer. Single-layer extrusion lines are simpler and more cost-effective, producing tubes with uniform properties. On the other hand, multi-layer extrusion lines are more advanced, capable of producing tubes with different layers of materials for enhanced strength, barrier properties, and aesthetics. Both types cater to different market demands, with single-layer lines being more common in simpler applications and multi-layer lines in more specialized applications.</p></p>
