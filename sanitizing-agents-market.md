<p><p> Sanitizing agents market is divided into two main types: non-oxidizing disinfectants and oxidizing disinfectants. Non-oxidizing disinfectants include alcohols, aldehydes, amphoterics, phenolics, quaternary ammonium compounds (QACs), and others. These agents work by disrupting cell membranes and denaturing proteins. On the other hand, oxidizing disinfectants like peracetic acid and hydrogen peroxide work by releasing oxygen radicals that destroy microbial DNA. Both types of sanitizing agents are important for maintaining clean and safe environments in various industries.</p></p>
