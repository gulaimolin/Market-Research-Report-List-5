<p><strong>The Pressure Pool Cleaners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Booster Pump Powered Cleaner</li><li>Filter Pump Powered Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure pool cleaners come in two main types: booster pump powered cleaners and filter pump powered cleaners. </p><p>Booster pump powered cleaners require a separate booster pump to provide the necessary pressure to operate efficiently. This type of cleaner is ideal for larger pools or those with heavy debris.</p><p>On the other hand, filter pump powered cleaners rely on the existing pool pump and filter system to create the pressure needed for operation. This type of cleaner is easier to install and maintain, making it a popular choice for smaller pool owners.</p></p>
