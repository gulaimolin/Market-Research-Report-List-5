<p><p>Metal finishing machines are categorized into various types based on their specific functions and applications. Deburring machines are used to remove sharp edges or burrs from metal surfaces. Polishing and buffing machines are utilized to enhance the appearance of metal components by smoothing and shining their surfaces. Grinding machines are designed to remove material from metal surfaces through abrasive actions. Other types of metal finishing machines include shot blasting and honing machines, each serving different purposes in the finishing process.</p></p>
