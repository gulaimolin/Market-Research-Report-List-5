<p><strong>The Pelletizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strand Palletizing System</li><li>Underwater Palletizing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pelletizer market includes two main types: Strand Palletizing System and Underwater Palletizing System. Strand Palletizing System is a method where pellets are formed into strands and then cut into desired lengths before being palletized. This system is ideal for handling a wide range of materials and is known for its high efficiency. On the other hand, Underwater Palletizing System involves pellets being cooled and solidified underwater before being palletized. This system is commonly used for thermoplastic materials and offers precise control over pellet size and shape.</p></p>
