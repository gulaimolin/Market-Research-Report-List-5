<p><p>Styrenic polymers market types include Polystyrene (PS), which is a versatile and cost-effective material used in various applications. Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) offers high impact strength and heat resistance. Styrene Acrylonitrile (SAN) provides transparency and chemical resistance. Methacrylate Butadiene Styrene (MBS) enhances impact strength and weatherability. Methacrylate Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (MABS) combines the properties of ABS and MBS. Styrene Methyl-Methacrylate (SMMA) offers transparency and weather resistance. Styrene Butadiene Styrene (SBS) and Styrene Ethylene Butylene Styrene (SEBS) are elastomeric materials with excellent flexibility and impact resistance. Other types of styrenic polymers offer unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
