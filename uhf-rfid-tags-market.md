<p><p>UHF RFID Tags market consists of two main types: UHF Dry Inlay RFID Tags and UHF Wet Inlay RFID Tags. UHF Dry Inlay RFID Tags are essentially RFID chips attached to a substrate, while UHF Wet Inlay RFID Tags are RFID chips encased in a protective layer of adhesive. The main difference lies in their durability and suitability for different applications. Dry Inlay tags are more durable and suitable for use in harsh environments, while Wet Inlay tags are more cost-effective and commonly used in retail and logistics industries.</p></p>
