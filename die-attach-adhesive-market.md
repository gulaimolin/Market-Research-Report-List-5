<p><ul><li>Die Attach Paste</li><li>Die Attach Film</li><li>Die Attach Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die attach adhesive market consists of three main types: Die Attach Paste, Die Attach Film, and Die Attach Wire. Die Attach Paste is typically used in applications where high thermal conductivity and good adhesion properties are required. Die Attach Film is a pre-cured adhesive film that is easier to handle and dispense compared to paste. Die Attach Wire involves using fine wires to attach the die to the substrate. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on specific requirements and preferences in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
