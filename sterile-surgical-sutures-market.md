<p><ul><li>Sterile Absorbable Surgical Yarns</li><li>Sterile Nonabsorbable Surgical Yarns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sterile surgical sutures are divided into two main types: sterile absorbable surgical yarns and sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns. Sterile absorbable surgical yarns are designed to be dissolved and absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal. These are commonly used in internal surgeries where long-term support is not needed. On the other hand, sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns are designed to remain in the body without being absorbed, providing long-term support for wound closure. These are typically used in surgeries where extended support is required.</p></p>
