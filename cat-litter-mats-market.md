<p><p>Cat litter mats can be classified into two main types - single layer and double layer mats. </p><p>Single layer cat litter mats consist of a single layer of material designed to trap litter as cats exit their litter boxes. These mats are usually easy to clean and maintain.</p><p>Double layer cat litter mats, on the other hand, consist of two layers - a top layer to trap litter and a bottom layer to prevent liquid from seeping through. These mats provide better protection against messes but may require more effort to clean.</p></p>
