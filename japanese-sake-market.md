<p><p>Japanese Sake market can be broadly categorized into five main types: Ordinary Sake, Junmai, Honjozo, Junmai Ginjo, and Ginjo. Ordinary Sake is the most common type, with a balanced flavor profile. Junmai Sake is made purely from rice, water, and koji without any added alcohol. Honjozo has a small amount of distilled alcohol added. Junmai Ginjo is a premium variety with a higher milling rate and complex flavors. Ginjo is the highest quality Sake with a delicate aroma and refined taste, often considered a luxury item.</p></p>
