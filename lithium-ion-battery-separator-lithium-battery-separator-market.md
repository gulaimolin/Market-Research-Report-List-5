<p><p>Lithium-Ion Battery Separator market consists of two main types: Bilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator and Trilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator. The Bilayer separator is made of two layers of polymer materials, providing basic thermal shutdown protection. On the other hand, the Trilayer separator includes an additional layer to offer enhanced safety features such as improved thermal stability and mechanical strength. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and performance of lithium-ion batteries in various applications.</p></p>
