<p><strong>The Photographic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Pass Filter</li><li>Short Pass Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photographic filter market types include Long Pass Filters and Short Pass Filters. Long Pass Filters allow longer wavelengths of light to pass through while blocking shorter wavelengths, resulting in a warmer, redder image. Short Pass Filters, on the other hand, allow shorter wavelengths to pass through while blocking longer wavelengths, producing a cooler, bluer image. Both types of filters are popular among photographers looking to control the color temperature and overall aesthetic of their images.</p></p>
