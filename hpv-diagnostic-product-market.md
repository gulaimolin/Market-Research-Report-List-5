<p><p>The HPV diagnostic product market includes various types of diagnostic technologies such as in situ hybridization, HC2 technology, and PCR. In situ hybridization is a technique used to detect specific HPV DNA sequences within cells. HC2 technology is a nucleic acid hybridization-based assay that detects high-risk HPV types. PCR (polymerase chain reaction) is a molecular technique used to amplify and detect HPV DNA. These different types of diagnostic technologies play a vital role in diagnosing and monitoring HPV infections.</p></p>
