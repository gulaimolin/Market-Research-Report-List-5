<p><ul><li>Type FC Spindle Oil</li><li>Type FD Spindle Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spindle oil is classified into two main types: Type FC and Type FD. Type FC spindle oil is formulated for use in high-speed spindle bearings exposed to high temperatures, providing excellent lubrication and thermal stability. On the other hand, Type FD spindle oil is designed for machines with a higher risk of fire hazards, offering superior anti-rust and anti-wear properties. Both types cater to different requirements in the industrial sector, ensuring optimal performance and protection for machinery.</p></p>
