<p><p>Flexible Graphite Bipolar Plate Market Types - Compression Molding and Injection Molding involve different manufacturing processes for creating bipolar plates used in fuel cells. Compression molding involves placing a mixture of flexible graphite and resin into a mold and applying pressure and heat to create the desired shape. Injection molding, on the other hand, injects molten flexible graphite resin into a mold under high pressure to form the bipolar plate. Both methods offer unique advantages in terms of cost-effectiveness, scalability, and production speed in the market.</p></p>
