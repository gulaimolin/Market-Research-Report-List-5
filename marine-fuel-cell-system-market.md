<p><p>Marine Fuel Cell System market includes two main types - Polymer Electrolyte Membrane Fuel Cell (PEMFC) and Solid Oxide Fuel Cell (SOFC). PEMFC operates at low temperatures and is suitable for small vessels due to its compact size and quick start-up time. On the other hand, SOFC operates at higher temperatures and is ideal for larger vessels due to its high efficiency and ability to use various fuel sources. Both technologies are being increasingly adopted in the marine industry for their clean and efficient power generation capabilities.</p></p>
