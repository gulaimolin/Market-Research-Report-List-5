<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The LED Etcher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Etcher</li><li>Wet Etcher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED etcher market can be categorized into two types: dry etcher and wet etcher. Dry etcher uses plasma to remove material from the surface of the LED substrate through chemical reactions, while wet etcher uses liquid chemicals to dissolve material. Dry etcher is preferred for high precision etching and small feature sizes, while wet etcher is commonly used for bulk material removal and larger feature sizes. Both types of etchers play a crucial role in the manufacturing process of LED devices.</p></p>
