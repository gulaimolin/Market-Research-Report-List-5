There are two main types of road cold recyclers in the market: Asphalt Emulsion Recyclers and Foamed Asphalt Recyclers. Asphalt Emulsion Recyclers use a mixture of asphalt emulsion and aggregate materials, while Foamed Asphalt Recyclers use foamed asphalt to rejuvenate and strengthen the existing road surface. Both types of recyclers are used to reduce waste and save costs in road construction projects.</p></p>
