<p><p>Flexo inks are used in the printing industry for flexographic printing. Solvent-based printing inks contain solvents that evaporate during the drying process, making them ideal for high-speed printing on various substrates. On the other hand, water-based printing inks use water as a solvent, making them more eco-friendly and suitable for food packaging applications. Both types of inks offer different advantages and are chosen based on specific printing requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
