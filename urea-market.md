<p><p>Large Granule Urea refers to urea fertilizers in the form of larger granules, typically used in agriculture for slow-release nitrogen supply. This type of urea is more suited for crops with longer growing periods. On the other hand, Small Granule Urea consists of smaller granules that release nitrogen faster, making it suitable for crops with shorter growing periods. Both types of urea offer different advantages and are used based on specific crop requirements and soil conditions in the agricultural market.</p></p>
