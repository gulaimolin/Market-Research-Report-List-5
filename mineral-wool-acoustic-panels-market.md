<p><p>Mineral wool acoustic panels are commonly divided into two main types: glass fiber wool and mineral wool. Glass fiber wool panels are made from molten glass spun into fibers and are known for their lightweight and high sound absorption properties. Mineral wool panels, on the other hand, are made from natural rock or slag materials and are praised for their fire resistance and durability. Both types of panels are popular choices in the market for their ability to effectively improve sound quality in various spaces.</p></p>
