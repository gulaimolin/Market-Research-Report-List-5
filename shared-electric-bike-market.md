<p><p>Point to Point market refers to a system where users pick up electric bikes from designated stations and drop them off at another station. This model is common in cities with centralized docking stations. Distributed market, on the other hand, allows users to pick up and drop off bikes at any location within a specified area, using a smartphone app for payment and tracking. This model is more flexible and convenient for users but requires technology like GPS and IoT to manage the bikes efficiently.</p></p>
