<p><ul><li>Powder Soil Amendment</li><li>Granular Soil Amendment</li><li>Liquid Soil Amendment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soil amendments are products used to enhance the fertility and productivity of soil. There are three main types of soil amendments on the market: powder, granular, and liquid. Powder soil amendments are finely ground and easy to mix into the soil. Granular soil amendments are larger particles that gradually break down over time. Liquid soil amendments are diluted in water and applied directly to the soil. Each type offers unique benefits and can be chosen based on the specific needs of the soil and plants.</p></p>
