<p><strong>The FIBC (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Container) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type A FIBCs</li><li>Type B FIBCs</li><li>Type C FIBCs</li><li>Type D FIBCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs) come in various types depending on their level of safety for handling flammable materials. Type A FIBCs are made of regular polypropylene and offer basic protection against static electricity. Type B FIBCs have antistatic properties but are not designed for use in flammable environments. Type C FIBCs feature conductive fabrics and are safe for use in flammable atmospheres with the proper grounding. Type D FIBCs, on the other hand, eliminate the need for grounding as they are constructed from static dissipative fabrics.</p></p>
