There are two main types of carotid stents: Balloon Expandable Stents, which are placed using a balloon catheter to expand the stent in place, and Self-Expanding Stents, which use a spring-like mechanism to expand the stent once it is in position. Both types of stents are used to improve blood flow in the carotid artery and reduce the risk of strokes.</p></p>
