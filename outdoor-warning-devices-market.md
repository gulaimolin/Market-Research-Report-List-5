<p><p>Outdoor warning devices can be classified into three main types: Omni Directional, Rotating, and Directional. Omni Directional devices emit a 360-degree sound pattern, ensuring coverage in all directions. Rotating devices have a spinning mechanism that allows the sound to be projected in different directions periodically. Directional devices focus the sound in a specific direction, ideal for targeting a specific area or audience. Each type caters to different needs and environments, providing versatility and effectiveness in warning and communication systems.</p></p>
