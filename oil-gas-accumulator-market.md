<p><ul><li>Bladder</li><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil Gas Accumulators are used to store pressurized fluid in hydraulic systems for emergency power backup. There are three main types of Oil Gas Accumulators: Bladder, Diaphragm, and Piston. Bladder accumulators consist of a flexible bladder separating the fluid and gas sections. Diaphragm accumulators use a flexible diaphragm to separate the fluid and gas sections. Piston accumulators use a moveable piston to separate the fluid and gas sections. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
