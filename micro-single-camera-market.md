<p><strong>The Micro-single Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mirrorless System</li><li>Fixed Mirror System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro-single camera market is segmented into two main types - mirrorless system and fixed mirror system. Mirrorless system cameras are compact and lightweight, lacking a mirror for an optical viewfinder. They offer high-quality images and advanced features. Fixed mirror system cameras have a fixed translucent mirror that reflects light to the autofocus sensor while allowing light to pass through to the image sensor. They provide fast autofocus and continuous shooting capabilities. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of photographers.</p></p>
