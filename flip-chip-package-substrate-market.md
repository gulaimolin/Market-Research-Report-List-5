<p><strong>The Flip-Chip Package Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FCBGA</li><li>FCCSP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flip-Chip Package Substrate market includes two main types: Flip-Chip Ball Grid Array (FCBGA) and Flip-Chip Chip Scale Package (FCCSP). FCBGA is a type of semiconductor package that uses flip-chip technology and a ball grid array for interconnection. It offers high performance and reliability for advanced electronic devices. FCCSP, on the other hand, is a compact chip-scale package that also utilizes flip-chip technology. It is commonly used in portable electronic devices and offers a smaller form factor compared to FCBGA. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
