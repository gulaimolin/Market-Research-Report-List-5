<p><p>Lung tumor ablation market includes different types of technologies such as Radiofrequency Ablation (RFA), Microwave Ablation (MWA), Cold Ablation, and others. RFA uses electrical energy to heat and destroy tumor cells, while MWA utilizes microwave energy for the same purpose. Cold ablation involves freezing the tumor cells to kill them. Other technologies may include laser ablation or irreversible electroporation. These techniques offer minimally invasive alternatives to surgery for treating lung tumors, providing patients with potentially less pain and shorter recovery times.</p></p>
