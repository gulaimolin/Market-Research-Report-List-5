<p><p>Stage lift market can be categorized into three main types: platform lift, truss hoisting, and rotary lift. Platform lifts are used for elevating performers, equipment, or props on stage. Truss hoisting systems allow for quick and efficient vertical movement of lighting or sound equipment. Rotary lifts are circular platforms that rotate performers or objects on stage. Each type of lift serves a different purpose in the entertainment industry, providing versatility and creativity in stage design and production.</p></p>
