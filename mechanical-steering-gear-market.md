<p><p>The mechanical steering gear market consists of three main types: rotary, rack, and hydraulic steering systems. Rotary steering systems use a rotary motion to turn the wheels of a vehicle. Rack steering systems utilize a rack and pinion mechanism to convert rotary motion into linear motion to steer the wheels. Hydraulic steering systems use hydraulic fluid to assist in steering, making it easier for the driver to turn the wheels. Each type has its own unique advantages and is used in a variety of vehicles.</p></p>
