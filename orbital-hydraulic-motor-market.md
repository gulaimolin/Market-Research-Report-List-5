<p><p>Orbital hydraulic motors come in two main types: Fixed Capacity and Variable Capacity. Fixed capacity motors have a set displacement and operate at a constant speed, making them suitable for applications where a consistent output is required. In contrast, variable capacity motors allow for adjustment of displacement and speed, providing greater flexibility and control in various operating conditions. Both types are commonly used in industrial and mobile machinery for tasks such as driving conveyor belts, winches, and other equipment that require hydraulic power.</p></p>
