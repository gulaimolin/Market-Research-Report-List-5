<p><p>The automotive steer-by-wire system market includes two types: pure electronic and backup mechanical systems. Pure electronic systems rely solely on electronic components to steer the vehicle, offering advanced automation and precise control. On the other hand, backup mechanical systems incorporate a mechanical backup in case of electronic system failure, providing a reliable safety measure. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with pure electronic systems offering convenience and efficiency, while backup mechanical systems prioritize safety and reliability.</p></p>
