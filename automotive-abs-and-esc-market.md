<p><p>Automotive ABS (Anti-lock Braking Systems) and ESC (Electronic Stability Control) are two important safety features in modern vehicles. Three Channel ABS uses one sensor for each front wheel and one for the rear wheels, allowing independent control of the front and rear brakes. Four Channel ABS has individual sensors for each wheel, providing more precise control over each wheel's braking. ESC helps prevent skidding and loss of control by selectively applying brakes to individual wheels. These technologies enhance overall vehicle stability and safety on the road.</p></p>
