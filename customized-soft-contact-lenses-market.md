<p><p>Customized Soft Contact Lenses are available in two main types - Silicon Hydrogel and Hydrogels. Silicon Hydrogel lenses are made with a highly breathable material that allows more oxygen to reach the eye, resulting in increased comfort and eye health. Hydrogels, on the other hand, are made with a lower water content but are still designed for improved moisture retention and comfortable wear. Both types are popular choices for those seeking customizable and comfortable contact lens options.</p></p>
